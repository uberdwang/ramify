Graduating Senior

Must be a Senior graduating May 2016

College-Bound

Accepted to a four-year accredited university.

Deliverable: Submit copy of official letter of acceptance from a U.S. 4-year accredited university.

Essay

Deliverable: One essay (300 words or less). “Why should you receive this scholarship?”

GPA + Transcript

Minimum 3.0 weighted cumulative GPA as of Fall 2015 semester

Deliverable: Submit unofficial transcript (via email; can be sent by applicant or Linda Griffin in the career services office).

Letters of Recommendation

Teacher/counselor and peer

Deliverable: One Letter of Recommendation from Teacher or Counselor (submitted by teacher/counselor via email): “Please describe why the student deserves to receive the scholarship.”

Deliverable: One Letter of Recommendation from a peer (submitted by peer via email; 300 words or less): "Please describe one specific situation in which the candidate had a positive impact on either a team/community activity, class project, etc. Please describe in what context you know the student."

Extracurricular

Showcase your other side!

Deliverable: List of extracurricular activities (Word document). Include duration of involvement and position(s) held.
